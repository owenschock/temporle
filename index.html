How To Play

Triangulate the historical epicenter before your Integrity collapses.

  • Read the historical objective and type a modern-day country to scan.
  • The radar compass will point you toward the correct target.
  • Every wrong guess damages your Integrity. Farther guesses deal more damage.
Examples
Brazil8,204 km

Brazil is on the wrong continent. The radar points towards the target, but you take Heavy Damage (15%).

SpainCLOSE - CHECK BORDERS

Spain is within 800km! The radar blip turns orange, but you still take Light Damage (5%).

FranceTARGET FOUND

France is the exact location. The timeline is secured!
